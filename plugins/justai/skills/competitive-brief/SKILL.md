# Competitive Brief

Use this skill when campaign messaging should be shaped by a competitor or a
crowded category.

## Workflow

1. Define the competitive question.
   - Which competitor or category?
   - Which audience is comparing options?
   - What campaign is this meant to support?

2. Research the comparison.
   - positioning
   - pricing and packaging
   - message themes
   - weaknesses or gaps
   - claims that are risky to mirror

3. Turn research into campaign guidance.
   - core differentiation angle
   - supporting proof points
   - phrases to lean into
   - claims to avoid

4. Hand off to the right next skill.
   - `campaign-brief` when the campaign still needs a full brief
   - `deploy-campaign` when the brief is ready and the message just needs to be
     encoded in the script

## Output format

```text
Competitive context:
Primary differentiation angle:
Supporting proof points:
Messaging to avoid:
Recommended campaign hook:
Next step:
```
